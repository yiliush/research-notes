# CodeStory

CodeStory is a London-based Y Combinator S23 startup building Aide, an open-source AI-native IDE built on top of VS Code. The project sits awkwardly between the horizontal IDE category (Cursor, Windsurf) and the specialty repo-intelligence category — it's a full IDE but with a focus on autonomous agent loops rather than chat-driven coding.

## Origin

Founded in 2023 by Skcd (Sumit Kapoor) and team, YC S23 batch, based in London. Mistral AI is among the disclosed investors per Crunchbase, suggesting European AI ecosystem ties.

## Funding & Traction

- Pre-seed via YC S23.
- Mistral AI listed as investor.
- Specific round sizes not heavily disclosed.
- Open-source repo `codestoryai/aide`: ~2,200 stars (archived as of latest snapshot).
- Active development continues in `codestoryai/sidecar` (the AI brain that pairs with Aide).

## Product

- **Aide.** VS Code fork with native AI integration — autonomous agent that can make multi-file changes, plan and execute tasks.
- **Sidecar.** Rust-based local AI brain that powers Aide. Runs alongside the IDE.
- **Privacy-first design.** Code stays local where possible.

## Pricing

OSS core; commercial pricing not extensively published.

## Differentiators

- **VS Code-native, not a separate app.** Lower switching cost than Cursor.
- **European/UK base.** Different talent pool and regulatory positioning than SF-centric competitors.
- **Mistral AI backing.** Aligns with the European open-weight ecosystem.
- **Privacy framing.** Stronger than Cursor's cloud-first defaults.

## Risk factors

- Aide repo is marked as a public archive — suggests OSS development has paused.
- Competing against [[Cursor]] / Windsurf / Claude Code with vastly less capital.
- Hybrid open-source + commercial model is hard to execute without a strong lead product.
