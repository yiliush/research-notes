# Bito

Bito is a code review specialist competing in the second tier of the category — below CodeRabbit and Greptile in funding and revenue, but with steady customer growth and a sharply lower price point. The company emphasizes codebase awareness and offers reviews in 50+ programming languages with one-click fixes.

## Origin

Founded in 2022 by Amar Goel and Anand Das (both ex-PubMatic founders), based in San Francisco. Bito started as a generic AI coding assistant before pivoting to PR review as the sustainable wedge.

## Funding & Traction

- Total seed-stage funding: ~$8.8M.
- 2025: $5.7M seed extension led by Vela Partners, with NGP Capital, NextView, Maxitech, Eniac.
- Cited metrics: 89% faster PR merges, 87% of review feedback AI-generated, $14 return per $1 spent (customer-reported, vendor-published).

## Product

Bito's AI Code Review Agent integrates with GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket. Features:

- Line-by-line PR feedback.
- Issue detection, performance optimization, one-click fixes.
- Codebase awareness via repository indexing.
- 50+ language support.
- IDE plugins (VS Code, JetBrains) for review-before-push.

## Pricing

- Team: $15/user/month.
- Professional: $25/user/month.
- Enterprise: custom.
- 14-day free trial, no credit card required.

The $15 entry price is roughly half of [[CodeRabbit]] and [[Greptile]].

## Differentiators

- **Price.** $15/user is among the lowest in the category for a managed AI reviewer.
- **Quiet execution.** No founder drama, no PR wars — Bito just ships and acquires.
- **Founder experience.** Goel/Das previously built PubMatic (IPO'd 2014), so enterprise sales motion is more mature than YC-stage peers.

## Risk factors

- Pricing race-to-the-bottom while incurring real LLM inference costs.
- No clear technical moat distinguishing from [[CodeRabbit]], [[Qodo Merge]], or [[Korbit]].
- Small funding rounds relative to category leaders — risks getting outspent on go-to-market.
