# Locofy

Locofy is a Figma-to-code (and Penpot-to-code) platform built around Large Design Models — purpose-trained models for converting design files into production-grade front-end code across React, React Native, Vue, Angular, Flutter, Next.js, and HTML/CSS. The February 2024 one-click LDM-powered conversion launch is what moved Locofy from a power-user plugin into a more mainstream design-to-code option.

## Origin
- Founded 2021 by Honey Mittal, Sohaib Muhammad, and Hannan Saleem. Singapore-headquartered with significant remote engineering presence.
- Backed by Accel, Northstar Ventures, others; raised a Series A in 2023.
- LDM (Large Design Models) brand launched February 2024 — model class trained on design-code pairs rather than generic LLMs.

## Adoption
- 100,000+ designers and developers cited on the company site.
- Active Figma plugin presence; published Penpot integration.
- Heavy usage among design agencies and product teams that want to bridge Figma handoff without rebuilding by hand.

## Product
- Figma and Penpot plugins for design-to-code conversion.
- Multi-framework output: React, React Native, Vue, Angular, Svelte, Next.js, Flutter, HTML/CSS.
- Tagging — turn static layers into interactive functional elements (buttons, inputs, video, iframe). LocoAI suggests tags automatically.
- Live prototypes — Locofy can spin up a fully functional prototype without code, then export to the chosen framework.
- LDMs — the proprietary models trained specifically for design-to-code; positioned against generic LLMs that struggle with layout fidelity.

## Pricing
- Pay-as-you-go: $0.40 per LDM token.
- Starter: $399/year ($0.27/token).
- Pro: $1,199/year ($0.20/token).
- Enterprise: custom, bulk discounts, unlimited tokens.
- All plans billed annually.

## Differentiators
- LDM positioning — Locofy is the loudest advocate that design-to-code needs purpose-trained models, not general LLMs. The marketing hinges on this distinction; the actual quality gap vs Builder.io's Mitosis-based pipeline is contested.
- Broadest framework matrix in the category — Flutter coverage in particular is stronger than Anima or Builder.io.
- Tagging workflow is the most developed interactive-element pipeline among Figma-to-code tools.
- Pricing-by-token is friction for individual users vs Anima's flat $24/month.
- Less momentum than Builder.io on the AI-IDE-handoff story — Locofy's narrative is still "design to production" rather than "design through Cursor/Claude into the codebase."
