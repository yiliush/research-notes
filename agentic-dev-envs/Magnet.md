Based on the available secondary references, Magnet appears to lean toward:

- **Multi-agent orchestration** rather than a single autonomous agent — coordinating several agents on different tasks or different parts of a project from one interface.
- **Parallel task execution** as the headline pattern.
- **Single-pane management UI** for human oversight of the fleet.

## Differentiators

Conditional on the orchestration framing being accurate:

- **Orchestration-first.** If positioned as a meta-layer over agents (rather than an agent itself), Magnet competes with Claude Squad and tmux-based parallel coding harnesses rather than directly with Devin/Jules.
