# Market Landscape

The Agentic Development Environment (ADE) market in mid-2026 is the fastest-scaling category in B2B software history. Cursor went from $0 to $2B ARR in roughly 36 months. Lovable added $100M of revenue in a single month. GitHub Copilot crossed broad enterprise saturation. The category bifurcated into incumbent hyperscaler platforms (Copilot, AWS Q/Kiro, Google Antigravity), pure-play IDE/agent startups (Cursor, Cognition+Windsurf, Replit, Augment), open-source flank (Aider, Cline, OpenHands, Goose, Continue), and vibe-coding adjacencies (Lovable, v0, Bolt). Capital concentration is extreme: more than $25B in private equity flowed into ADE startups across 2025-26.

- **GitHub Copilot** — ~20M total users (July 2025), ~4.7M paid subscribers (Jan 2026, +75% YoY), 50,000+ organizations, ~90% Fortune 100 penetration (GitHub, Microsoft FY reports).
- **Cursor / Anysphere** — $2B ARR (Feb 2026), in talks to raise $2B at $50B pre-money (TechCrunch, Apr 2026). Prior round: $2.3B at $29.3B post-money (CNBC, Nov 2025). Forecasting $6B ARR by EOY 2026.
- **Lovable** — $400M ARR (Feb 2026), 8M users, 146 employees, $6.6B valuation (TechCrunch, Dec 2025 / Mar 2026).
- **Replit** — $9B valuation, $400M Series D (Mar 2026), targeting $1B ARR by EOY 2026 (TechCrunch).
- **Cognition** — $10.2B valuation post-Windsurf acquisition (CNBC, Sep 2025); combined enterprise ARR up >30% post-deal.
- **Claude Code** — Anthropic's coding agent runs ~$13/dev/active day, $150-250/dev/month in enterprise accounts; ships only on Team Premium ($100/seat, 5-seat min), Enterprise, Pro, and Max.
- **OpenAI Codex** — ~4M weekly active developers as of GPT-5.5 launch (Apr 23, 2026).

## Structural Observations

The market split into "everyone who replaces the IDE" (Cursor, Windsurf-now-Cognition, Augment) and "everyone who runs alongside the IDE/terminal" (Claude Code, Codex CLI, Cline, Aider, Goose). Vibe-coding (Lovable, v0, Bolt, Replit Agent) is structurally distinct — it sells to non-engineers and converts prompts directly into deployed apps.

Hyperscaler position: Microsoft owns Copilot's incumbency and enterprise contracts. Google reverse-acquihired Windsurf execs for $2.4B then folded its agent stack into Antigravity. AWS sunset Q Developer (new signups blocked May 15, 2026) and is migrating to Kiro. Each hyperscaler is now distributing its frontier model through both a first-party IDE/agent and an API consumed by every third party in this list.
