# Agentic Development Environments

A map of content for understanding the market, architecture, and frontier of coding agents — the tools that write, review, debug, and operate software with varying degrees of autonomy. The term "Agentic Development Environment" was coined by [[Warp]] with the Warp 2.0 launch in June 2025; this vault uses it as the umbrella for the whole category.
