# Research Vault: Agentic Development Environments

This is an Obsidian-style wiki of 175+ interlinked markdown notes mapping the Agentic Development Environment (ADE) market — the tools that write, review, debug, and operate software with varying degrees of agent autonomy. Covers technical primitives, foundational companies, market landscape, infrastructure, models, and frontier categories as of late May 2026.

The term "ADE" was coined by [[Warp]] with the Warp 2.0 launch (June 2025) and is used here as the umbrella for the full category.

## Where to Start

- **[[Agentic Development Environments MOC]]** is the map of content. Start here for any question.
- **[[Market Landscape]]** for funding, ARR, GitHub stars, and structural overview.
- **[[Market Structure and Value Chain]]** for the six-zone segmentation that organizes the vendor space.
- **[[Inner Loop vs Outer Loop]]** for the single most useful frame for understanding why the market split the way it did.
- Notes use `[[wiki-links]]` — follow them to navigate.

## Structure

- **Surface notes** (e.g., `AI-Native IDEs.md`, `CLI Coding Agents.md`, `VSCode Ecosystem.md`) are cluster indexes that link to the entity notes in that surface.
- **Entity notes** (e.g., `Cursor.md`, `Devin.md`, `Lovable.md`) cover one company or product each with funding, architecture, adoption, pricing, differentiators.
- **Primitive notes** (e.g., `Plan Mode.md`, `Subagents.md`, `Hooks.md`, `Diff-Based Editing.md`) explain one architectural concept each.
- **Market notes** (`Market Landscape.md`, `Competitive Dynamics.md`, `The Windsurf Saga.md`, etc.) cover the business landscape.
- **Infrastructure notes** (`Cloud Sandboxes.md`, `E2B.md`, `Model Context Protocol (MCP).md`) cover the substrate beneath agents.
